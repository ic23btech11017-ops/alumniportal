About the Event

Join us for an evening of networking and celebration as we honor the achievements of our distinguished alumni. The Annual Engineering Excellence Gala is our college's premier event, bringing together industry leaders, faculty, and graduates from across the decades.

This year, we will be unveiling the new Robotics Research Wing and presenting the "Innovator of the Year" award. Dinner and drinks will be served, followed by a keynote address on "The Future of Sustainable Infrastructure." Don't miss this opportunity to reconnect with old classmates and forge new professional relationships.
